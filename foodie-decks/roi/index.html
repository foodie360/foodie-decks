What is ROI in marketing?

ROI stands for Return on Investment. In marketing, it answers: for every $1 you put in, how many dollars come back? But before calculating returns, every business owner needs to answer one question first: how many guests do I need just to break even? That's exactly what this calculator shows — using your own numbers.
